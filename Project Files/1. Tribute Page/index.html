13 Interesting Facts about A. P. J. Abdul Kalam

1. Kalam was 11th president of India and interestingly he never owned a television. Some of his personal possessions include a veena, books, a CD player, a laptop and some articles of clothes.

2. We know Kalam sir was a Muslim by religion, but he was well aware of all Hindu traditions.

3. APJ Abdul Kalam also served as Chief Scientific Advisor to the Prime Minister during 1992-99.

4. Kalam was very hardworking man. At the age of 10, he started selling newspaper to support his family.

5. Very few peoples knew that Kalam was India’s first bachelor president, and he was also a vegetarian.

6. It would surprise you that Kalam has received 48 honorary doctorates from Indian and Foreign Universities and institutions.

7. Kalam was the man who is responsible for development of Prithvi and Agni missiles, which made him ‘Missile Man’ of India.

8. Kalam was awarded the highest civilian award of India- Bharat Ratna in 1997. He was also awarded coveted civilian awards- Padma Bhushan (1981), Padma Vibhushan (1990) and many other honorary awards.

9. APJ Abdul Kalam’s autobiography “Wings of Fire”: first published in English language and later it was translated into 13 languages including Chinese and French. There are many other biographies by other authors on his work and life.

10. Did you know? Drypetes kalamii, a newly discovered plant species was named after him by scientists from the Botanical Survey of India in February 2018.
